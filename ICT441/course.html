Fundamentals of Nursing

This course introduces the basic concepts, principles, and skills of nursing, including nursing ethics, nursing practice skills, and patient safety.

Nursing Management and Leadership

This course focuses on developing students' management and leadership skills, including nursing team management, healthcare resource allocation, and crisis management.

Clinical Nursing Practice

This course helps students master clinical nursing skills through hands-on and simulated scenario training, including infusion, dressing changes, and nursing assessment.

Nursing Research Methods

This course introduces the basic methods and techniques of nursing research, develops students' scientific research skills, and helps them conduct scientific research in nursing practice.

Cross-cultural nursing

This course explores nursing practice and patient needs in different cultural contexts to help students understand and respect differences between cultures and to enhance intercultural nursing.

Mental health care

This course introduces the theory and practice of mental health nursing, develops the student's ability to recognize and deal with mental health issues, and enhances the quality of nursing services.

Chronic care

This course focuses on the care needs and management practices of patients with chronic illnesses and helps students understand the characteristics of chronic illnesses and care strategies to provide more effective care.
