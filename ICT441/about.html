Nursing is a comprehensive discipline dedicated to the preparation of professional caregivers who play a key role in the healthcare environment. Nursing focuses not only on the physical health of patients, but also on taking care of their mental health and social needs. Through the study of Nursing, students will be equipped with a wealth of medical knowledge and nursing skills that will enable them to provide comprehensive nursing care to patients and facilitate their rehabilitation and health maintenance.

The field of nursing covers a wide range of areas, including clinical nursing, nursing management, nursing research and many others. During their study in Nursing, students will receive systematic theoretical training and practical guidance to develop solid nursing skills and professionalism. The development of Nursing is not only related to the development of the medical industry, but also involves the progress of social public health and human health, and is crucial to the improvement of the quality and efficiency of the entire healthcare service system.

Nursing emphasizes comprehensive care and aims to improve the overall health of the patient. Nursing professionals need to be keenly observant, patient and empathetic, in addition to possessing excellent medical knowledge and skills. Their job is not only to provide medical care to patients, but also to provide care and support to help them through the difficult moments of illness. The core concept of nursing is to be patient-centered, to enhance the physical and mental health and quality of life of patients through professional nursing services, and to contribute positively to the health of the society.
